Weekly Plan Overview

Day Workout Focus
Day 1Chest + Triceps
Day 2Back + Biceps
Day 3Legs (Quads, Hamstrings, Calves)
Day 4Shoulders + Abs
Day 5Upper Body Strength (Push/Pull)
Day 6Full Body HIIT or Functional
Day 7Rest / Recovery

Day 1: Chest + Triceps

  • Bench Press – 4 sets x 8–10 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 3 x 10
  • Chest Fly – 3 x 12
  • Triceps Pushdowns – 3 x 12
  • Skull Crushers – 3 x 10
  • Triceps Dips – 3 x 12
  • Optional: Pushups – 2 sets to failure

Day 2: Back + Biceps

  • Pull-Ups – 3 x 8
  • Lat Pulldowns – 3 x 10
  • Bent Over Barbell Rows – 3 x 8
  • Seated Cable Row – 3 x 12
  • Barbell Curls – 3 x 10
  • Hammer Curls – 3 x 12
  • Preacher Curl – 3 x 12

Day 3: Legs

  • Squats – 4 x 8
  • Leg Press – 3 x 12
  • Walking Lunges – 3 x 10 (each leg)
  • Romanian Deadlifts – 3 x 10
  • Leg Curl Machine – 3 x 12
  • Standing Calf Raises – 3 x 15
  • Seated Calf Raises – 3 x 15

Day 4: Shoulders + Abs

  • Overhead Barbell Press – 4 x 8
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raises – 3 x 12
  • Front Raises – 3 x 12
  • Rear Delt Fly – 3 x 12
  • Shrugs – 3 x 15
  • Plank – 3 x 30–60 sec
  • Leg Raises – 3 x 15
  • Cable Crunch – 3 x 15

Day 5: Upper Body Strength (Push/Pull)

  • Deadlifts – 4 x 6
  • Weighted Pull-Ups – 3 x 6–8
  • Weighted Dips – 3 x 6–8
  • Incline Barbell Press – 3 x 6–8
  • Barbell Rows – 3 x 8
  • Barbell Curls (Heavy) – 3 x 6–8

Day 6: Full Body HIIT

  • 10 Burpees
  • 15 Kettlebell Swings
  • 20 Box Jumps
  • 15 Battle Rope Slams
  • 10 Dumbbell Snatches (each arm)
  • 15 Air Squats
  • Repeat 3–5 rounds (rest 1 min between)

Tips

  • Warm-up 5–10 mins before each session
  • Stretch 5–10 mins post-workout
  • Hydrate properly
  • Adjust weights and reps based on your goal
